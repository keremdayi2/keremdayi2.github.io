About me

I am currently a 4th year undergraduate at Harvard College, working towards an A.B. in Computer Science and Mathematics and an S.M. in Computer Science. I am interested in foundational problems in important applications. Outside of work, I am very involved with music as the bass guitarist of the band ‘When in Doubt’ and play the electric and bass guitars (+ a little bit of piano).

I was fortunate to be able to work on many research problems as an undergraduate. Currently, I am working on rigorously understanding low-rank fine-tuning and am advised by Prof. Sitan Chen. Previously, I worked on resilience and distributed optimization in multi-agent systems, advised by Prof. Stephanie Gil and Prof. Angelia Nedich.
